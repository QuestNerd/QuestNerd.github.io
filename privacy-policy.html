Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026.

This site (QuestNerd) is a static website served from GitHub Pages. It does not run a backend, does not set first-party tracking cookies, and does not store any data server-side. The sections below describe the limited data flows that happen as you browse.

Information we collect directly

We do not collect personal information from you unless you choose to send it (for example, by emailing hello@questnerd.com or by signing up for the newsletter). Email you send us is used only to reply to you.

Third-party services

  • GitHub Pages hosts this site and may log basic request metadata (IP address, user-agent, timestamp) for abuse detection. See GitHub's privacy statement.
  • Stripe handles all payments through Stripe-hosted Checkout. Card details never touch this site. Stripe's privacy terms apply during checkout — see stripe.com/privacy.
  • Cults3D, Etsy, and Google Play handle their own listings and checkouts. When you click through to them, their respective privacy policies apply.

Cookies

QuestNerd does not set tracking or analytics cookies on this site. Third-party services you navigate to (Stripe, Etsy, Cults3D, Google Play) may set their own cookies under their own domains; those are governed by their privacy policies.

Your rights

You can request a copy or deletion of any personal data you have sent to QuestNerd by emailing hello@questnerd.com.

Changes to this policy

Updates are committed to the public repository for this site. The "last updated" date above reflects the most recent change.
