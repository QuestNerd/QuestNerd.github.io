About QuestNerd

QuestNerd is a one-person studio for makers, players, and tinkerers — a hub that combines high-quality 3D printed products, digital STL/OBJ models, original Android apps and games, and the project portfolio behind it all.

Everything you see here is designed, printed, coded, photographed, and shipped by a single engineer who genuinely enjoys finishing things. The store, the apps, the models, and even this website are part of an ongoing build-in-public experiment.

What you'll find here

  • 3D Models — digital STL files on Cults3D, and physical prints on Etsy.
  • Apps & Games — published on Google Play.
  • PC Downloads — software, tools and resources, with secure Stripe Checkout for paid items.
