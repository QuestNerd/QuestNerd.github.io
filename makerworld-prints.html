How it works

1. Pick a print-time tier

Every model on MakerWorld lists its estimated print time. Pick the tier that covers it: 1 hour or less, 1 to 6 hours, 6 to 12 hours, or 12 to 24 hours. If the print is longer than 24 hours or has unusual requirements, message me first for a custom quote.

2. Choose a filament color

Standard PLA colors are included in the base price. Premium colors (silks, gradients, exotic blends) carry a small upcharge that's added on at checkout.

3. Paste the MakerWorld URL

I only accept links on makerworld.com. Paste the URL for the exact model (and specific profile/variant, if relevant).

4. Pay through Stripe, then I print & ship

You'll be handed off to Stripe for secure checkout. The order details — including your MakerWorld URL and chosen filament — come straight to me. Shipping is calculated by Stripe at checkout. I'll email you a tracking number once it ships.
