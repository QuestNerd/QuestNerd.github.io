Shipping & Returns

Last updated: 2026.

Physical prints (Etsy)

Physical 3D prints are produced and shipped through QuestNerd on Etsy. Each listing on Etsy displays its own processing time, shipping options, and price by destination. Etsy's buyer protection and return policy apply.

  • Domestic and international shipping is available on most items.
  • Hand-printed items typically ship within 3–7 business days after the order is placed.
  • If a print arrives damaged, contact us through the Etsy order page within 7 days and we'll send a replacement or refund.

Digital 3D models (Cults3D)

Digital STL/OBJ files are delivered instantly via Cults3D after checkout. Because the goods are digital, all sales are final except in cases of corrupted or missing files — in which case we'll resend the file or refund the purchase.

PC downloads (this site, via Stripe)

Paid PC downloads sold through Stripe Checkout are delivered by email (or via a direct link returned after successful payment). As with digital models, all sales are final except in cases of a corrupted, missing, or undelivered download. In that situation, email hello@questnerd.com with your Stripe receipt and we'll resend the file or issue a refund.

Android apps (Google Play)

Apps and games are distributed by Google Play. Refunds and returns for purchases or in-app purchases follow Google Play's standard policy.

Questions?

Email hello@questnerd.com with your order number (Etsy, Cults3D, Google Play, or Stripe receipt) and we'll sort it out.
