Skelepet spotlight

A cozy skeleton-pet Android prototype with a tiny undead buddy, short care loops, and a deliberately simple black-and-white style.

Skelepet is planned as a Tamagotchi-style game where the character starts in an egg and grows through food, sleep, play, wellness checks, unlockable backgrounds, and Skelecoins.

The scope is intentionally focused: lightweight screens, readable icons, short sessions, and minigames like tic-tac-toe, hangman, and a slot machine. The goal is fun daily interaction without aggressive streak pressure.
