Skelepet spotlight

Skelepet is a cozy pet-care experiment where players collect bones, craft outfits, and keep a tiny undead buddy happy through short daily check-ins.

The design goal is "low-pressure progression": sessions are 2–5 minutes, with lightweight goals and no punitive streak systems.
